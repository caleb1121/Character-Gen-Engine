==========================================================================
                WELCOME TO THE REALITY ENGINE (v1)
==========================================================================

Reality Engine is a lightweight, JSON-driven framework designed to build 
deep, logically consistent characters and entire universes from the 
ground up. It acts as a "Universal Character Engine," allowing 
you to create everything from Cyberpunk mercenaries to Eldritch horrors 
simply by editing text files.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
🚀 WHAT CAN THIS APP DO?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Reality Engine is more than a simple randomizer; it is a logic-driven 
simulation of identity and history. Here is what it offers:

* **Deep Identity Logic**: Manages a three-part naming system (First, 
  Last, and optional Nickname) that respects gender requirements.
* **Gender & Pronoun Resolution**: Automatically rolls gender and 
  assigns "Smart Pronouns" ({Subject}, {Object}, {Possessive}) for 
  use in biographies.
* **The "Tag" System**: Acts as the engine's brain, ensuring traits 
  make sense (e.g., a "Laser Sword" requires an "Augmented" tag).
* **Weighted Rarity Control**: You define exactly how rare an item is. 
  Weight 100 is common; Weight 1 is a legendary find.
* **RPG Stat Balancing**: Uses "Point Pools" to mathematically 
  distribute points across custom attributes like Str, Dex, or Int.
* **Narrative Storytelling**: Stitches together biography fragments 
  into a cohesive life story based on the character's rolled data.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
💎 THE "SMART" BIO SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

The standout feature of Reality Engine is its "Stat-Aware" narrative 
engine. This allows for "Stat-Gating," where specific story 
sentences only trigger if a character is strong, smart, or lucky enough 
to "earn" them through their rolled attributes.

* **Trait Injection**: Placeholders like {Starting Weapon} are 
  automatically swapped for the actual items the character rolled.
* **Tag-Based Branching**: A character's origin can change entirely 
  based on tags like "Noble-Blood" or "Pauper".

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
🛠️ HOW TO LEARN
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

This library is organized into levels to help you master the engine:

1. **Level 1**: Basic Randomization (Simple lists).
2. **Level 2**: Weights & Rarity (Simulating scarcity).
3. **Level 3**: Logic & Stats (Tags and RPG math).
4. **Level 4**: Narrative Elite (Stat-gated storytelling).
